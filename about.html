About Trusty

Our mission is to make digital safety simple and accessible for everyone

Our Story

Trusty was created by a team of digital safety experts and educators who saw a growing need for clear, friendly guidance on navigating the online world safely. We noticed that many older adults feel left behind by technology, yet they're increasingly targeted by sophisticated scams.

That's why we developed this course: to empower everyone with the knowledge they need to protect themselves online, presented in a way that's welcoming, not intimidating.

Our team has decades of combined experience in cybersecurity, education, and user experience design. We've worked with thousands of people to understand the real challenges they face online and developed practical solutions to address them.

Our Approach

At Trusty, we believe that digital safety education should be:

  • Clear and jargon-free - We explain concepts in plain language
  • Practical and relevant - We focus on real-world scenarios you're likely to encounter
  • Empowering, not frightening - We help you feel confident, not scared
  • Accessible to everyone - Our content is designed with all users in mind
